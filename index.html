About Us

In HMRITM, it is a ritual that we select a Web Development Team every year from our Computer Science & Engineering Department. These are the students who have shown tremendous interest and zeal in web development. These teams of seniors & juniors, are not only experienced in their work but also follow the work ethics required to be successful in any industry. In this way we promote a healthy culture and environment for students to teach and learn new technologies and help each other to grow together.

Besides being competitive developers these students are also the PROFESSIONALS of other fields as well. With this note we introduce to you, some of our best selected web developers below
